Who We Are

Coderic is a for-profit Software Factory operating in Latin America. We develop software solutions at both small and large scales. Our vision is oriented toward people, not large proprietary products. We create economical software that benefits individuals, with most of our offerings being free and open source. Our proprietary software is primarily Software as a Service, which helps position and improve our commercial image.

What is Coderic Organization?

Coderic Organization is not a cooperative nor a foundation. While we may establish these entities in the future, that is not what we currently do. Instead, we draw on cooperative principles as the easiest way to explain to Latin American developers how they can join and participate in Coderic Organization and how they can benefit from our infrastructure and technological ecosystems.

Our model ensures that developers are never exploited or laborally abused. We operate through a mutual benefit mechanism where:

  • Coderic supports developers in developing their projects and entrepreneurial ventures
  • We provide tools to help bring projects to successful completion
  • Developers retain their intellectual property rights
  • Fair benefit distribution: Coderic receives sales commissions from developer software, which are even lower than typical sales commission rates

This cooperative-style framework helps us explain our model clearly to developers across Latin America, ensuring they understand how to participate, benefit from our infrastructure, and collaborate in a mutually beneficial way.
